Welcome to

Kryfton Consulting Inc.

Kryfton brings a refreshing, passionate, and personalized approach to sourcing talent for your growing team. We act as an extension of your Human Resources and leadership team, quickly a solid understanding of the  unique role requirements. We then pair these with your company culture and growth objectives to drive a targeted talent search for strong, dynamic, diverse, and experienced level candidates. We understand that a solid recruiting process is critical to bringing you the very best people. We’re  prepared to help you scale with candidates that take care of their well-being and their business. We symbolize ourselves by the quality of our services and the quality of our deliverable.

As organizations constantly strive to iterate their business and IT strategies, Kryfton services to define, optimize and align clients’ business strategy with technology initiatives.

We spend quality of time and resources screening and hiring top quality resources. This allows us to provide finest services that meets and exceed our clients’ expectations. Clients benefit from seamless coordination across strategy, implementation, and management of their technology programs; and from our expertise in focused industries, strong quality orientation, cross-technology expertise, and distributed.

Where many consulting and talent solutions providers are focused on “the transaction," we believe in establishing and maintaining long-term partnerships so that we can meet your strategic needs and enable your organization to glean greater business value over time.
